## Why this level matters (lineage)

**Classical root:** deliberate practice — a concept formalized by K. Anders Ericsson in the 1990s but recognizable in apprenticeship traditions stretching back centuries.
**Modern descendant:** every serious self-directed learning program — fast.ai, CS231n study groups, the DeepLearning.AI specializations — treats structured, staged, hands-on practice as non-negotiable. Road to AI is just that, gamified.
