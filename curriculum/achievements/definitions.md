# Achievement Definitions

Each entry: id (kebab-case, platform-facing), title (human-facing), trigger (when it fires), level (rarity tier).

## first-step
- **Title:** First Step
- **Trigger:** Complete any level
- **Level:** common

## met-10-luminaries
- **Title:** Met 10 Luminaries
- **Trigger:** Encountered 10 distinct luminary spotlights
- **Level:** uncommon

## turing-award-trio
- **Title:** Met the Turing Trio
- **Trigger:** Encountered luminary spotlights for Hinton, LeCun, AND Bengio
- **Level:** rare

## backprop-by-hand
- **Title:** Derived Backprop by Hand
- **Trigger:** Completed F25 (Math Boss)
- **Level:** rare

## first-paper-reproduced
- **Title:** First Paper Reproduced
- **Trigger:** Completed any Track B level at P36+ (Raw tier)
- **Level:** rare

## glossary-trailblazer
- **Title:** Glossary Trailblazer
- **Trigger:** Encountered 25 distinct glossary terms via chips
- **Level:** uncommon

## recall-champion
- **Title:** Recall Champion
- **Trigger:** Completed 10 Recall Challenges
- **Level:** uncommon
